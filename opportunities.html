Master's Students at UMD

We're always looking for highly motivated students who are interested in the lab's research to work with us. You may do an independent study for course credit under CMSC798 if you are in CS (Master's Non-Thesis Research), or INST799 if you are in INFO. Alternatively, when funding permits, we may offer paid research positions.

If you are interested, please fill out this form.


Undergraduate Students at UMD

Undergraduate students can perform an independent study for course credit under CMSC298A, CMSC498A, or CMSC499A. If you want to work with us on a research project, please keep in mind of the following requirements:

  • A commitment to work for at least 6 hours per week (except midterms and finals weeks) for at least one entire semester
  • Meet weekly to report and discuss research progress
  • Be willing to work with other undergraduate students and potentially Ph.D. students as a team

If you are interested, please fill out this form.
